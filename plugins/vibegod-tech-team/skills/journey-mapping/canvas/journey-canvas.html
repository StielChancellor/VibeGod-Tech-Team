Navigate & edit

Move around: mouse-wheel to zoom (toward the cursor), drag empty space to pan, Fit to frame everything.

Connect: hover a step and drag its pink ● handle onto another step. (Or Connect / press C on a focused step.)

Keyboard: Tab focus · ↑↓←→ move · Enter edit · Esc cancel · Del delete.

Readability: only the happy path shows by default. Click a step's ▸ badge, or Show detail, to reveal error/loading/empty states.

Hand back: Copy JSON → paste into the chat (the team treats it as approved). Download/Load for backups.
